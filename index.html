Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a prescription?
    Tap the + button on the home screen. You can scan a prescription card with your camera or enter the values manually.
  • What does SPH, CYL, Axis, and ADD mean?
    Open any prescription record and tap the info icon next to the values. Lens Passport explains every value in plain language.
  • How does the contact lens conversion work?
    Open a glasses prescription, tap the menu (···) and choose "Convert to Contact Lens". Lens Passport uses the HSA formula to calculate the correct contact lens values.
  • Is my data private?
    Yes. Your data is stored on your device and synced privately via iCloud. No data is shared with third parties.
  • How do I export a PDF?
    Open a prescription record and tap the share icon in the top right corner.

Privacy Policy
